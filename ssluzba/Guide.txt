

To properly install Lombok Library, follow the next steps.

1. Go where your Lombok jar is - 
	C:\Users\User\.m2repository/org/projectlombok/lombok/1.16.10/lombok-1.16.10.jar
	run it. A window should appear.
	browse to your eclipse or sts.exe location
	
2. Click on Install

3. Launch or restart Eclipse, clean and rebuild project, then right click on your project, 
	maven -> update project.
